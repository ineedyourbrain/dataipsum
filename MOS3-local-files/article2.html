Information, Tickets and Tours!!!

You may not love your military ID mug shot, but when you realize how much it can do for you, you might just warm up to it! Did you know that your ID gets you access to:

  • Discounts on hotels, car rentals and vacation packages
  • Savings on day trips to nearby cities and attractions
  • Deals on local attractions like sporting events, brewery tours, movie tickets and more Through your local Information, Tickets and Tours office, service members and their families can access these discounts and much more. Click here to read about all of the benefits.
  • Whether you long to explore faraway cities or discover new spots in your own backyard, Information, Tickets and Tours has you covered. You have access to discounts around every corner for family trips or solo flights.

What savings can I get through Information, Tickets and Tours?

Plan

a bit of outdoor adventure into your next move or family vacation with the Best Kept Secrets Joint Services Campgrounds and Facilities.

Your military ID gives you access to discounts on the following:

  • Amusement park tickets
  • Sporting event tickets
  • Concerts and shows
  • Adventure trips
  • Museums and historic sites
  • Vacations
  • Day trips
  • Hotels
  • Car rentals
  • Campsites
  • Brewery/winery tours
  • Movie tickets and more!

Why should I book and purchase through Information, Tickets and Tours?

Booking and purchasing through the ITT office offers the following benefits:

  • Discounts (duh!)
  • Exclusive offers for service members
  • Avoidance of additional fees
  • Efficient, personalized service
  • Trip-planning services

Many ITT personnel act as travel agents. They can work out the details and the best savings for your trip, saving you time AND money. Whether you’re destined for Vegas or Disney, the staff can get you there at the best rate.

Who is eligible for these services and discounts?

The following service members are eligible for services:

  • Active-duty go to another site
  • National Guard and retired Guard members under 60 years of age
  • Reserve component and retired reserves under 60 years of age
  • Family members of the above-listed service members
  • Department of Defense civilians
  • Nonappropriated fund personnel
